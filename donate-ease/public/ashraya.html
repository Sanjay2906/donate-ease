ASHRAYA FOUNDATION

Welcome to Ashraya Orphanage Home

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing shelter, education, and healthcare to orphans and underprivileged children in our community. Our mission is to improve the lives of these children and empower them to break the cycle of poverty. What We Do: - Provide a safe and nurturing environment for children - Offer educational programs to help children reach their full potential - Deliver healthcare services to ensure the well-being of our children - Foster a sense of community and belonging for our children How You Can Help: - Make a donation to support our programs and services - Volunteer your time and expertise to help us achieve our mission - Spread the word about our organization and the work we do

Our Mission

Ashraya Orphanage Home, our mission is to improve the lives of orphans and underprivileged children in our community. We believe that every child deserves access to education, healthcare, and a safe and nurturing environment. Our goal is to empower these children to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for themselves and their communities

Our History

Ashraya Orphanage Home was founded in 2005 by a group of concerned citizens who saw a need for a safe haven for orphaned and abandoned children. Since then, we have grown to become one of the leading non-profit organizations in our community, serving hundreds of children each year.

Our Services

Shelter

We provide safe and comfortable living arrangements for children in need of shelter. Our homes are staffed by caring professionals who provide support and supervision 24/7.

Education

We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. That's why we offer a variety of educational programs to our children, including tutoring, computer skills training, and vocational training.

Donate

Your donations are essential to helping us provide the best possible care and support to our children. You can make a one-time or recurring donation through our website or by contacting us directly.
