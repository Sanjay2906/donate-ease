Welcome to AMMA Orphanage Home

At Amma Foundation orphanage, we believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive. Our dedicated team of staff and volunteers work tirelessly to provide a supportive and loving environment for our children, and we are proud to have helped hundreds of children over the years. Impact Statistics: .Over 500 children have been provided with a safe and caring home. .95% of our children go on to complete their education and secure employment. .Our programs have helped to reduce the number of child laborers in the surrounding area by 50%. OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing home for orphaned and abandoned children, while also empowering them with the education and life skills necessary to lead successful and fulfilling lives.

OUR HISTORY

Amma Foundation orphanage was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate individuals who wanted to make a difference in the lives of orphaned and abandoned children. Over the years, we have grown to become a leading organization in the field of child welfare, with a team of dedicated staff and volunteers who are committed to our mission.

OUR VALUES

Compassion

We are committed to treating every child in our care with kindness, respect, and empathy. We believe that every child deserves to be loved and cared for, and we work tirelessly to ensure that our children feel safe, secure, and supported.

Excellence

We are committed to providing the highest quality care and services to our children. We strive for excellence in everything we do, and we hold ourselves to the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.

Collaboration

We believe that working together is essential to achieving our goals. We collaborate with our staff, volunteers, donors, and partners to ensure that we are providing the best possible care for our children and making a positive impact in our community.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering our children with the education, skills, and resources they need to succeed in life. We believe that every child has the potential to achieve great things, and we are committed to helping them realize their full potential.

Transparency

We are committed to being transparent in our operations and communications. We believe that our donors and supporters have a right to know how their contributions are being used, and we strive to provide regular updates and reports on our activities and impact.

OUR SERVICES

Shelter

We provide a safe and secure home for orphaned and abandoned children, ensuring they have access to nutritious food, clean water, and a comfortable place to sleep.

Education

We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and empowering children to build a better future for themselves. We provide our children with access to quality education, including formal schooling and vocational training.

Healthcare

We prioritize the health and well-being of our children, and provide access to medical care and treatment as needed.

Emotional Support

Our staff and volunteers provide emotional support to our children, helping them to process the trauma of losing their families and to build trusting relationships with caring adults.

Recreational Activities

We believe in the importance of play and recreation, and offer a variety of fun and engaging activities for our children to enjoy. This includes sports, games, arts and crafts, and field trips.
